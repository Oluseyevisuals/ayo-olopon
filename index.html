How to Play

1

The board has 2 rows of 6 pits. Each starts with 4 seeds.

2

On your turn, pick all seeds from one of your pits (bottom row).

3

Distribute seeds counter-clockwise, one per pit.

4

If the last seed lands in an opponent pit with 2 or 3 seeds, capture them!

5

Keep capturing backwards if previous opponent pits also have 2 or 3.

6

First to capture 25 seeds (majority) wins the round!

7

Win rounds to rise through Yoruba titles — all the way to Oba! 👑
