Privacy Policy — Ayo Olopon

Last updated: 23 March 2026

Ayo Olopon is a free traditional Yoruba board game developed by Oluseyevisuals. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

Ayo Olopon does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The game runs entirely in your browser and all data (game progress, title rank, win/loss record) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements. Third-party ad networks may collect certain non-personal data (such as device type and general location) to serve relevant ads. Please refer to the privacy policies of those ad networks for more information.

Children's Privacy

Ayo Olopon is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Third-Party Services

The game is hosted on GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for information about how GitHub handles data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
