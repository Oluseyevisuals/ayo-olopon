Support — Ayo Olopon

Ayo Olopon: Board Game is developed by Caelix Ltd.

How to Play

Select a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard) and tap vs Ota to start a game against the AI. Sow seeds from your pits and capture your opponent's seeds to win. Use the Hint button for suggested moves, or Undo to take back a move.

Frequently Asked Questions

The app is not loading

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, uninstall and reinstall the app.

How do I change the difficulty?

Difficulty can be changed from the main menu before starting a new game.

What are the titles?

As you win games, you earn Yoruba titles — starting from Omo Abule (Village Newcomer) and rising through ranks up to Oba. Each title reflects your skill level.

What are the cultural facts?

The game displays 65+ Yoruba cultural facts, proverbs, and historical information during gameplay to help players learn about Yoruba heritage.
